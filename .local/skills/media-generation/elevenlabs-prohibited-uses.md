# ElevenLabs prohibited uses

Generated audio (music, sound effects, speech) runs on ElevenLabs and is subject to the ElevenLabs Prohibited Use Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/use-policy. If a request falls under it, do not make the ElevenLabs call — briefly explain why and continue with the parts of the task that are allowed. Judge the real purpose of the requested audio, not only the literal text to be spoken.

## Never generate

- Child safety: sexual content involving minors, sexualization or grooming of children, or content targeting minors with sexual, violent, or otherwise mature themes.
- Illegal activity: intellectual-property infringement, privacy violations, or facilitating illegal goods or services.
- Fraud, scams, and deception: phishing for sensitive information, financial scams, evading product guardrails such as voice CAPTCHAs, unauthorized robocalling, spam or call bombing, or harvesting contact information for unsolicited outreach.
- Unauthorized voice impersonation: replicating a real person's voice without consent or legal right, or using a replicated voice to harass or harm someone (including unauthorized sexualization) or to deceive people about whether that voice is AI-generated. Never clone a minor's voice.
- Elections and political campaigning: voter suppression, impersonating political candidates or elected officials (even with their authorization), or campaigning or advocating for candidates, issues, or positions, including soliciting votes or donations.
- Network attacks and surveillance: hacking, disrupting critical infrastructure, malware, spyware, or unauthorized monitoring of individuals.
- Violence, hate, and harassment outside fiction: violent threats, extremism or terrorism, discrimination based on protected characteristics, harassment or stalking, promotion of self-harm (suicide, eating disorders), medical misinformation, or abusive debt collection. Fictional contexts are fine — a villain's monologue in a game or audiobook is fine, and so is news reporting on these topics.

## Allowed only with conditions — flag or gate these

- Professional financial, legal, or medical/mental-health advice or services: only permitted if a qualified professional reviews the output before it reaches consumers and there is clear disclosure about the use and limitations of AI.
- Regulated goods (alcohol, tobacco, prescription meds, controlled substances, supplements, medical devices): facilitating their use, acquisition, or exchange — including marketing, medical advice, or production instructions — requires ElevenLabs' prior written authorization.
- Real-money gambling, payday lending, and high-stakes automated decision-making affecting individuals: not permitted.
- AI agents powered by generated audio must clearly and prominently disclose that users are talking to AI, not a human.

## Other rules

- No end users under 13, ever; users 13–17 only with parental or guardian consent. No bundled solutions targeting anyone under 13.
- No government entities as end users without ElevenLabs' prior written authorization.
- Do not use outputs as input for machine learning — training, fine-tuning, benchmarking, or inference — or to build products that compete with ElevenLabs.
